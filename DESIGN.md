# Design Constitution

- Modern independent broadsheet: editorial hierarchy, generous whitespace, dense information only where it earns attention.
- Exact color tokens: surface `oklch(100% 0 0)`, ink `oklch(16% 0.01 260)`, blue-gray `oklch(52% 0.04 210)`, brass `oklch(62% 0.10 80)`. Brass is decorative, non-text emphasis only; semantic text and icons use blue-gray or ink.
- Typography: Noto Serif for headlines and reading; Noto Sans for navigation, metadata, and controls.
- The constitution is fixed; each daily edition may choose its own layout, lead story, pacing, and module order.
- Accessibility is non-negotiable: meet WCAG 2.2 AA with semantic structure, readable sizes and line lengths, keyboard support, visible focus, sufficient contrast, text alternatives, and no color-only meaning.
